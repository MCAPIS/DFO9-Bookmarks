To change all of the links to work for your station (which will carry over to the other pages), simply set your station name (either here or in the black navigation bar at the top of every page):

What is this?

I put together this list of handy links originally for myself, because there wasn't a readily-available list anywhere, and at the time I never had a dedicated laptop and couldn't just rely on bookmarks. (plus, bookmarks weren't helpful when I had to share links with other people)

I kept putting more and more links in here (primarily so I wouldn't lose them), and then more and more people at my station started using the page, eventually becoming the de facto resource for freshly-minted ambassadors. I eventually redesigned the website from the ground up to allow it to work with other stations (and to give it more room to expand further), which is when the site really exploded in usage.

The main features of the site are dynamically-updating report links (the links for today's dates are always updated for today, no matter what day it is), as well as being able to configure all of the links to work for your station.

I also have one-click links for dispatch tools (rather than clicking the link, fixing the date, selecting your site, etc.), quick links for PPA, Stow, and TOT reports, readily-available manifests for the most common dispatch cycles, and links to frequently-needed Apollo audits (along with direct links for today's and yesterday's results for those audits). I can also include custom links that exclusively appear only for the specified station.

If there's anything you think is missing here, feel free to reach out to me (preferably on Chime). I'm always looking for more stuff to add.

Stats: As of November 15th, there are 342 unique links on this page (not including any station-exclusive links), supporting 413 specific stations (though hundreds more are able to use this website as-is).
